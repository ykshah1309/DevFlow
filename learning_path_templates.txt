Junior Developer Template (14 days):
Week 1: Foundations (Project context, data layer, business logic, testing)
Week 2: First contribution (Domain prep, coding, code review)
Est. hours per day: 4-6 hours

Mid Developer Template (7 days):
Week 1 compressed: Architecture patterns, core modules, testing (4 days)
Week 2: First contribution (3 days)
Est. hours per day: 6-8 hours

Senior Developer Template (3 days):
Day 1: Codebase scan, identify key modules
Day 2: Deep dive core business logic only
Day 3: Take on first issue independently
Est. hours per day: 6-8 hours

Official Documentation Sources (APPROVED ONLY):
JavaScript: nodejs.org, developer.mozilla.org, expressjs.com
Python: python.org, docs.djangoproject.com, flask.palletsprojects.com
Ruby: ruby-lang.org, guides.rubyonrails.org
Databases: postgresql.org, mongodb.com/docs, dev.mysql.com
Testing: jestjs.io, mochajs.org, docs.pytest.org

Red Flags (alert mentor):
- Setup broken after Day 2
- Can't understand README after 4 readings
- Stuck on same file > 4 hours
- No PR opened by Day 12 (Junior) / Day 7 (Mid) / Day 3 (Senior)
